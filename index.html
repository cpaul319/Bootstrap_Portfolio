About me

I started down that road in the late 90s but instead took a different path into journalism. Its only recently that I discovered coding boot camps and thought they were perfect for my particular situation. Almost one month later, January 16th 2018, I had a massive heart attack. Although I survived, it damaged my heart severely and I started the process of regaining my strength. I look forward to changing careers and I know after what I have been through this year that nothing will stand in my way.
